History

Instant Solutions was established as a comprehensive advisory company specializing in tourist visa procedures for various countries. Recognizing the increasing demand for clear, honest, and straightforward guidance in the visa application process, our founders embarked on this journey in 2023.
Since our inception, we have remained committed to providing personal, orientation-focused, and customer-centric services. Our primary mission is to guide clients truthfully and objectively, ensuring strict adherence to the specific requirements and conditions of each country's embassy or consulate.
Over the years, we have fine-tuned our approach, using advanced technology, and accumulating invaluable experience in the field of visa advisory services. Through our dedicated Call Center and an intuitive online portal, we now offer our automated, nationwide services, catering to the unique needs and preferences of our clients, making the visa application process convenient and comfortable.
